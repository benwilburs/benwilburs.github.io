This is my personal website. It is currently under construction, but I intend to fill it out with various content related to my interests and activities.

In the Languages section, you will find some information about various languages I have studied over the years, in particular I intend to fill out the subsections on Classical Chinese and Classical Japanese, two languages I am currently trying to learn.

Under Westraliana, you may find various things about my home state of Western Australia. I am currently working on a project to collect the poems of Oscar Walters (1889-1948), a WWI war poet and labour activist whose poetry was once a regular feature of such publications as the Westralian Worker and The Bulletin.

Photos will take you to a gallery of some of the photos I have taken over the years. The better ones, at least.
